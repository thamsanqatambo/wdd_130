Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate and inspire visitors by showcasing the diverse world of music, including genres, instruments, artist spotlights, and music theory basics. The site aims to engage users through multimedia content, curated playlists, and beginner-friendly resources that promote a deeper appreciation and understanding of music.

Audience

The primary target audience for this site includes teenagers and young adults (ages 13–25) who have a growing interest in music—whether as casual listeners, aspiring musicians, or students looking to expand their musical knowledge. The site also serves educators or parents seeking introductory material for younger learners.
