Overview

Purpose

This website showcases my personal taste in music, featuring curated playlists, favorite genres, and artist spotlights. It's a place to share recommendations and inspire others to explore new sounds.

Audience

This site is intended for music lovers, students looking for study or workout playlists, and anyone wanting to discover new music in a clean and curated way.

Home Page

Welcome to my music site! Here you’ll find curated content that reflects my love for music across different moods and settings—from relaxing at home to training hard in the gym.

Genres

In this section, explore my favorite genres like Hip-Hop, Jazz, Classical, and Electronic. I’ll break down each genre and give artist and album recommendations for both new listeners and seasoned fans.

This page will feature playlists I personally created for different activities and moods like “Study Flow,” “Jiu-Jitsu Drive,” “Chill Sunday,” and more. Each playlist includes a short description and embedded Spotify link.
