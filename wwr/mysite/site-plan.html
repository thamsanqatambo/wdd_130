Overview

Purpose

This website showcases my personal taste in music, featuring curated playlists, favorite genres, and artist spotlights. It's a place to share recommendations and inspire others to explore new sounds.

Audience

This site is intended for music lovers, students looking for study or workout playlists, and anyone wanting to discover new music in a clean and curated way.

Content

Home Page

Welcome to my music site! Here you’ll find curated content that reflects my love for music across different moods and settings—from relaxing at home to training hard in the gym.

Images for the Home page

Genres

In this section, explore my favorite genres like Hip-Hop, Jazz, Classical, and Electronic. Each genre will be explained in detail with historical context, notable subgenres, and artist recommendations. From smooth saxophones to glitchy beats, there's something for every mood and moment.

Images for the Genres page

Playlists

This page will feature playlists I personally curated for different activities and moods like “Study Flow,” “Jiu-Jitsu Drive,” and “Chill Sunday.” Each playlist will include track lists, a short description, and embedded Spotify or YouTube links for direct listening.
